About Our Organisation

KIBALE WELFARE & EDUCATION FOUNDATION LIMITED(KWEF) is an indigenous nonprofit organization based in Mid-Western Uganda region. With its head office in Kamwenge District, File No. MIA/NB/2022/10/5326 Plot 166 Kibale, Block 75, Nsorora cell, Kamwenge-Mbarara Road, Kamwenge District. KWEF has been in existencesince 2015 operating in western region (Kamwenge, Kitagwenda, Kasese,Kyegegwa, Kyenjojo, Kazo and Ibanda Districts). KWEF is a communal network facilitated by a group of volunteers who were concerned about the challenges faced by the western region, with a common vision and mission to defeat communal poverty. Each KWEF Member is determined to lead programmes, raise funds, advocate for key issues and communicate to the public in their communities. These efforts supported our life-changing work in four districts in fiscal year 2017. Around the world, KWEF works alongside a broad network of partners and allies to help rebuild and improve the lives of the most disadvantaged, with a particular focus on women and girls. In the world’s poorest communities, girls and women bear the brunt of poverty and are also the key to overcoming it.

About Our Organisation

OUR AIM

That “Kibale Welfare & Education foundation “becomes the leading Non-profit organization in the field of providing outstanding humanitarian services in Uganda according to the highest practices.


MISSION

To create a difference in the lives of Africa’s children, youth, orphans, widows, elders, disabled and communities devastated by poverty, illiteracy and diseases regardless of social, religious, economic and political background to improve their quality of life.


VISION

To create a world where every human being attains their given rights to survival, protection, development, health environment and the feeling of belongingness.


OUR TERMS

To offer relief in the form of financial, emotional and physical support, in order to meet the needs of people ravaged by disaster and other misfortunes. The foundation collects and distributes supplies to families, individuals and institutions.


OUR HUMBLE REQUEST

The demand for the services of the organization in terms of humanitarian support, school buildings, and shelters for the homeless and other humanitarian services has surpassed our capacity. We therefore invite upon any organization, well-to-do persons and well-wishers to join us in this humanitarian struggle to donate to these vulnerable communities. We are a trusted organization that delivers quality work and services with a humane approach.

OUR CORE PRINCIPLES

Below are our core principles:

Humanity

Dedicated to saving lives and ending poverty, we help people around the country at their most desire moments, save and protect their lives, alleviate suffering and protect human dignity.

Impartiality

We do not discriminate on the basis of race, colour, ethnicity, class, national origin, religion, gender, or political opinions.

Inclusiveness

We work for the incorporation of needs and perspectives of people from diverse backgrounds and value the contributions of each and every human being.

Voluntary Service

We are not profit oriented. We aim to promote peace, understanding, cooperation and solidarity through voluntary service.

Independence

We are autonomous and do not accept any political or ideological influence.

OUR VALUES

Below are our core values:

Sustainability

We work on developing sustainable solutions to address the most pressing needs of communities and leaving a lasting impact on the lives of human beings.

Innovation

We are dedicated to promoting novel and creative solutions which offer effective solutions to crises and vulnerabilities.

Transparency

We work for the incorporation of needs and perspectives of people from diverse backgrounds and value the contributions of each and every human being.

Partnership

We work together with partners to make fundamental changes in the living conditions of people in need and increase our capacity to address pressing human needs around the country.

OUR FOCUS

We put women and girls in the centre because we know that we cannot overcome poverty until all people have equal rights.

ORGANISATIONAL OBJECTIVES

1. To promote and improve access to quality education in vulnerable communities through construction of schools.
2. To promote and improve access to health and hygiene in vulnerable communities through construction of hospitals and staging healthy camps.
3. To improve the quality of life of vulnerable communities through construction of water sources and shelters for vulnerable.
4. To support orphans and vulnerable children through general welfare support, education and health sponsorship.
5. Poverty alleviation in marginalize community through skills training and providing start up kits to women an youth.
6. Advocating for people living with HIV/AIS, and fighting against sexual abuses, early marriages, and gender-based violence.
7. To put up income generating projects that will generate income to sustain the activities of the organisation.
8. To carry out any other activity that will be deemed to be important in achieving the organisation goals and objectives.


SOURCES OF FUNDING

1. Funders, members and well-wishers contributions in cash and in kind.
2. Community contributions mainly in terms of land and labour.
3. Donors from international relief organizations.
4. Agency funds from people who want charity work done on their behalf basing on the trust earned by the organization.
